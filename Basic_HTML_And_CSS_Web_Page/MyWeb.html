FAQ

How do I add a task?

To add a task, go to the Home section and enter your task in the input field, then click the 'Add Task' button.

Can I edit a task once it's added?

Yes, click the 'Edit' button next to the task, make your changes, and then click 'Save Changes'.

How do I delete a task?

Click the 'Delete' button next to the task you wish to remove.

How can I contact support?
